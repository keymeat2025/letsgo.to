For Organizers: How to Upload Your Competition Details

1

Step 1: Sign Up or Log In

If you don't have an account, sign up. If you already have an account, log in.

2

Step 2: Go to 'Create Competition'

Navigate to the 'Create Competition' section from the dashboard.

3

Step 3: Fill in Competition Details

Provide all the necessary details about your competition, including title, description, dates, and any relevant rules.

4

Step 4: Upload Media

Upload any images, videos, or other media related to your competition.

5

Step 5: Submit for Review

Once you've filled in all the details, submit your competition for review. Our team will review and approve it within 24-48 hours.
