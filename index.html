For Organizers: How to Upload Your Competition Details

1

Sign Up or Log In

If you don't have an account, sign up. If you already have an account, log in.

2

Go to 'Create Competition'

Navigate to the "Create Competition" under "For Organizers" section from the "Home"

3

Fill in Competition Details

Provide all the necessary details about your competition, including title, description, dates, and any relevant rules.

4

Upload Media

Upload any prospectus or banners related to your competition.

5

Make payment and Submit for Review

Once you've filled in all the details, make discounted amount payment submit your competition for review. Our team will review and approve it within 24-48 hours.
